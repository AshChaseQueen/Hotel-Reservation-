ACCESSIBILITY

Welcome to the LA DIVERTIDA, where we prioritize inclusivity and accessibility for all our residents. Our commitment to creating a welcoming environment extends to individuals with disabilities, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the full range of amenities and services we offer.

At LA DIVERTIDA, we have implemented a variety of accessibility features to accommodate residents with mobility challenges. Our facilities are designed with ramps, elevators, and wide doorways to ensure easy navigation throughout the premises. Additionally, we provide accessible parking spaces and pathways to enhance convenience for all.

Whether it is wheelchair friendly entrances, accessible restrooms, or designated seating areas, we strive to create an environment that is comfortable and accommodating for everyone. Our staff is trained to assist residents with disabilities, ensuring that they receive the support they need to fully participate in community activities and events.

Join us in creating a vibrant and diverse living environment that celebrates the uniqueness of every individual.
