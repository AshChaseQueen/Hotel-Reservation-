Frequently asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

To make a reservation, you can visit our website and use the online booking system, or you can call our customer service hotline. Our team will assist you in finding the perfect room for your stay.

What is the cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy allows you to cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled arrival without any charges. For cancellations made within 24 hours, a fee may apply.

What amenities are available at the hotel?

Our hotel offers a range of amenities including a fitness center, swimming pool, spa services, complimentary breakfast, and free Wi-Fi throughout the property.

Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer complimentary parking for all our guests. Valet service is also available for your convenience.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is at 12:00 PM, and check-out time is at 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer room service?

Yes, we provide 24-hour room service but it is chargeable.
